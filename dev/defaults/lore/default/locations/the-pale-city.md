# The Pale City

A coastal city built on white limestone cliffs, giving it the pale appearance that inspired its name. It is the primary setting of the story.

## Districts

- **The Heights**: Where the Council chambers and wealthy estates are located. The Archive building sits at the highest point.
- **Ashward**: A middle-class residential district known for its narrow streets and independent shops. Elena Vasquez lives here.
- **Dockside**: The harbor quarter, rough but vital to the city's trade economy. Home to warehouses, taverns, and Maren Kade's information network.
- **The Undercroft**: Tunnel systems beneath the city, remnants of pre-Founding construction. Officially sealed, but accessible through several known entry points.

## The Founding

The official history states the Pale City was founded three hundred years ago by settlers who discovered the empty limestone plateau and built upon it. The founding families — including the Thornfields — are celebrated in annual festivals.

However, evidence suggests the city existed in some form before the "Founding." The Undercroft tunnels are architecturally distinct from anything built in the last three centuries, and Elena's research points to an earlier civilization that was deliberately erased from the record.

## Current Politics

The city is governed by a Council of twelve, drawn from the established families. The Thornfield family currently holds three seats, giving them outsized influence. There is growing tension between the old families and the merchant class from Dockside, who demand representation.
