# The Archivist

The keeper of the Pale City's official historical records, known publicly only by his title. His true name, Aldric Senn, is known to very few. He has held the position for over forty years, appointed during the previous Council's tenure.

## Appearance

An elderly man, tall and thin, with white hair and pale blue eyes. He dresses in the traditional grey robes of his office and carries a silver key on a chain around his neck — the key to the Restricted Collection.

## The Restricted Collection

The Archive contains a sealed section known as the Restricted Collection. Access requires the Archivist's personal approval. It is said to contain documents from before the Founding, though the Archivist denies this. Elena Vasquez gained unauthorized access during her apprenticeship and claims to have seen pre-Founding texts.

## Motivations

The Archivist believes that certain historical truths, if widely known, would destabilize the Pale City. He sees himself as a guardian, not a censor. His loyalty is to the city's stability above all else, even above historical accuracy.

## Relationships

- **Elena Vasquez**: Former apprentice. He views her departure as a betrayal but privately worries she may be right about the Founding records.
- **The Council**: He serves them but does not answer to them. The Archive predates the current Council structure.
- **The Thornfield family**: He maintains careful distance. The Thornfield records in the Restricted Collection are the most heavily guarded.
