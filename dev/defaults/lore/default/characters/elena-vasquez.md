# Elena Vasquez

A former archivist's apprentice, now working independently to catalog the history of the Pale City. She is in her early thirties, with dark hair kept short for practicality and sharp brown eyes that miss little.

## Background

Elena trained under the Archivist for six years before leaving under disputed circumstances. She claims she left voluntarily after discovering the Archivist was selectively destroying records that contradicted the official history of the Founding. The Archivist maintains she was dismissed for unauthorized access to the Restricted Collection.

## Personality

Methodical and patient in her work, but fiercely stubborn when challenged on matters of historical truth. She has a dry sense of humor and a habit of quoting obscure texts in casual conversation. She trusts slowly but is intensely loyal once trust is established.

## Current Situation

Elena operates from a small apartment in the Ashward district, where she maintains her own private collection of copied documents. She is currently investigating discrepancies in the founding records of the Pale City, particularly around the role of the Thornfield family.

## Relationships

- **The Archivist**: Former mentor, now adversary. She still respects his knowledge but considers him morally compromised.
- **Maren Kade**: Close friend and information broker in the Dockside quarter. They meet weekly at the Copper Lantern tavern.
- **Councillor Rhen**: Secretly supplies Elena with access to Council archives in exchange for her discretion about certain records.
