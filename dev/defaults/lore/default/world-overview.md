# World Overview

This story is set in a secondary world with roughly early-industrial technology. No magic exists in this world — mysteries have mundane (if hidden) explanations.

## Key Themes

- The tension between historical truth and social stability
- Institutional power and who controls the narrative
- Loyalty vs. principle

## Tone

Grounded and realistic. Think historical fiction rather than fantasy. The city feels lived-in and politically complex. Violence exists but is not glorified. The story favors investigation, conversation, and moral ambiguity over action set-pieces.

## Timeline

The story takes place over approximately three months, beginning in late autumn as Elena discovers the first major discrepancy in the Founding records.
