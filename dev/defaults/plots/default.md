# Default Plot Arc

## Premise
A fragile alliance forms under pressure, only to fracture when the truth finally surfaces.

## Beats
- Introduce the fragile status quo and the cost of maintaining it
- Trigger an inciting incident that forces cooperation
- Escalate through competing loyalties, secrets, and external danger
- Reveal a betrayal or hidden truth that recontextualizes earlier choices
- Force a climactic decision with emotional and practical consequences
- Land on an aftermath that closes one arc while leaving room for continuation

## Character Arcs
- Lead character: from caution to commitment
- Primary counterpart: from guarded distance to vulnerable honesty
- Antagonistic force: from pressure at the edge to direct confrontation

## Tension Curve
Begin with unease, build through intimacy and mistrust in parallel, and peak at a moment where affection and survival are in direct conflict.
